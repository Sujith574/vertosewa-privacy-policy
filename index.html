Privacy Policy for Vertosewa

Last updated: November 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Vertosewa ("we", "our", or "us") operates an AI-powered assistant that helps users of Lovely Professional University (LPU) with information, support, and automated responses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect user data.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information unless provided voluntarily by users. We may process:

  • Messages sent by users through WhatsApp
  • Queries asked to the chatbot
  • Basic metadata provided by the WhatsApp API (phone number, message type, timestamps)

We do not collect passwords, financial data, or sensitive personal information.

3. How We Use Data

Your data may be used for:

  • Generating AI responses
  • Improving chatbot quality
  • Understanding usage patterns

No user data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any external company except:

  • Meta Platforms (WhatsApp API)
  • Groq AI (for processing messages)

5. Data Security

We use secure servers and encryption to protect your data. Only authorized administrators have access to logs and API data.

6. User Rights

Users may request:

  • Deletion of their chat data
  • Clarification of how data is used

Contact us anytime for data-related concerns.
